Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our shop website, select the products you want, then go to order page, and Fill in your shipping and payment details to confirm your order.

Can I track my order?

Yes. Once your order has been shipped, you’ll receive a tracking link via email or SMS. Use it to track your delivery status anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, Telebirr, and Cash methods depending on your location.

Can I return a product?

Yes. We offer a 3-day return policy on most items. Products must be unused and in their original packaging. Contact us for our support for easy returns.

Do you offer free shipping?

We offer free shipping on selected products or when your order exceeds a minimum purchase amount. Check our shipping policy for details.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our support team through the Contact Us page or by emailing us directly. We’re available 24/7 to assist you.

Getting Started

  1. Browse Ebro-Shop website chrome or others.
  2. Browse and select your desired products.
  3. Complete your purchase through checkout.
  4. Track your order and enjoy by your items!

Technical Support

If you face any technical problems, please try the following steps:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • Try a different web browser.
  • Ensure a stable internet connection.
  • Disable browser extensions that may interfere.
  • Update your browser to the latest version.

If the problem continues, please reach out through the Contact Page.
